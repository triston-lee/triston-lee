## About

> I like things that are both precise and expressive.
>
> 我喜欢那些既准确，又有表达力的东西。

I work across code, research, and visual taste.

我在代码、研究和视觉表达之间来回穿梭。

I am currently a CS PhD student at HKUST CSE, and I spend most of my time thinking about graph machine learning, large language models, and how to build ideas into something clear and usable.

现在的主要身份是 HKUST CSE 的 CS PhD student。平时关注图机器学习、大语言模型，也关心如何把一个想法真正做成清晰、好用、好看的东西。

Before research, I also spent time learning art, so I care about structure, rhythm, and visual balance more than most engineers probably do.

在进入研究之前，我也认真学过美术，所以相比一般工程师，我会更在意结构、节奏和视觉上的分寸感。
